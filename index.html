About The Program

Every day so many children are being abandoned by their beloved ones. So many children are being left in the dustbins because their parents could not afford children.Some people are leaving their children who are suffering from dangerous diseases in orphanage homes.Some children are orphans by birth.The reasons are many but we have to take care of those.We are maintaing a home for those children.The main motto of this program is to take care of those kind of children by your support.This program is to take care of the children and to save the children by providing good education, health and livelihood in turn making their lives blossom.And we ensure that each and every penny you donate will not be mis-used at any cost.

Mission

  • Education

    • We work to ensure children are educated for life through collective efforts.

  • Health

    • We work to address the causes of malnutrition with special focus on children below 5 years

  • Livelihood

    • Our livelihood interventions enable families to enhance income and provide better for their children.
